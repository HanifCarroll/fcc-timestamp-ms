Timestamp Microservice

This app allows you to look up a human-readable date and get the unix timestamp, and vice versa!

How to use:

Simply place the cursor at the end of the URL, type a backwards slash (/) then input either the unix timestamp or human readable date (spaces work!), and press enter!

Examples:

https://pacific-crag-60929.herokuapp.com/1234567890
https://pacific-crag-60929.herokuapp.com/February%2013,%202009
